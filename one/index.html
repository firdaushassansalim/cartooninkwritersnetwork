    About Us

    Cartoon Ink Writers Network is a community of writers created by young and innovative writers, mainly to serve as a support system, nurturing and guiding both ongoing and prospective writers while equipping them with all the basic and necessary guidance for being success authors.
    We believe in the power of community contribution and team work, that is why we support each other, so we can grow together.

    Our Mission

    Our mission is to equip vibrant stars nurture their creative potential, while guiding them towards achieving and crushing their set goals.

    Our Vision

    Our vision is to make it easier for writers to explore the world through the power of their imagination, inked down on paper without having the usual writer’s block.

    Our Focus

    Our main focus is to increase a writers value to himself, his work and the community. We value writing as a powerful art and writers should be valued.
